# EmpathAI Datasets

The following datasets were used for the EmpathAI project, sourced from Hugging Face:

1. MentalHealthChat Dataset by Hizardev
   Link: https://huggingface.co/datasets/hizardev/MentalHealthChat

2. Dataset 2 
   Link: https://huggingface.co/datasets/Kanakmi/mental-disorders

3. Dataset 3

5. Dataset 5

6. Dataset 6 
   Link: https://huggingface.co/datasets/jsfactory/mental_health_reddit_posts

7. Dataset 7 
   Link: https://huggingface.co/datasets/Riyazmk/mentalhealth

8. Dataset 8 
   Link: https://huggingface.co/datasets/alexandreteles/mental-health-conversational-data

9. Dataset 9 
   Link: https://huggingface.co/datasets/mpingale/mental-health-chat-dataset

These datasets are publicly available and were used to train and evaluate the NLP models within the EmpathAI project.
